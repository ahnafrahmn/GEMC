SKILL MIGRATION SERVICES

We are dedicated to guiding you through every aspect of your migration process.

Our migration services include:

Skill Assessment:

  • Assistance in evaluating and assessing the individual's skills, qualifications, and work experience.
  • Guidance on choosing the appropriate skill assessment authority for the specific migration destination.

Occupation Matching:

  • Identification of occupations in demand in the target country based on the individual's skills and qualifications.
  • Assistance in selecting an occupation that aligns with the individual's expertise and the needs of the destination country.

Points Calculation:

  • Evaluation of eligibility based on points systems used for skilled migration.
  • Detailed analysis of how different factors, such as age, language proficiency, and work experience, contribute to the overall points score.

Documentation Support:

  • Guidance on preparing and organizing the necessary documentation for the skill migration application.
  • Assistance in obtaining certified copies of educational certificates, professional licenses, and other required documents.

Application Preparation:

  • Support in completing and submitting the skill migration application accurately and within specified deadlines.
  • Review of application materials to ensure compliance with immigration requirements.

Language Proficiency Assistance:

  • Recommendations for language proficiency tests (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL) if required for migration.
  • Preparation resources and guidance to achieve the necessary language scores.

Job Search Assistance:

  • Strategies for job searching in the destination country, including resume building and cover letter writing.
  • Ongoing support for any issues related to academics, visa compliance, or personal matters.

Pre-Departure Briefing:

  • Information and advice on the cultural, social, and professional aspects of living and working in the new country.
  • Assistance with travel arrangements and accommodation.

Legal and Immigration Compliance:

  • Guidance on understanding and complying with immigration laws and regulations.
  • Regular updates on changes in immigration policies affecting skilled migration.

Post-Arrival Support:

  • Ongoing support for individuals after they have migrated, including assistance with settling into the new environment.
  • Information on local resources, services, and communities that can aid in the transition.

Continuous Updates:

  • Regular updates on the status of the migration application.
  • Timely communication regarding any changes in immigration policies or procedures.
