VISA PROCESSING SERVICES

Our consultancy provides comprehensive assistance at every stage of the visa application process, increasing your chances of a successful outcome.

Our visa processing supports include but not limited to:

Initial Consultation:

  • In-depth discussion to understand the client's visa needs, including the purpose of travel, destination, and type of visa required.

Visa Category Selection:

  • Assistance in determining the most appropriate visa category based on the client's purpose, such as tourist, student, work, or family reunification visas.

Documentation Guidance:

  • Comprehensive checklist of required documents for the chosen visa category.
  • Detailed guidance on obtaining and organizing necessary documentation, including any notarization or authentication requirements.

Application Form Assistance:

  • Support in completing and reviewing visa application forms to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Guidance on payment methods and ensuring proper documentation of transactions.

Visa Fee Handling:

  • Assistance in understanding and managing visa fees, including any associated processing or service charges.
  • Guidance on payment methods and ensuring proper documentation of transactions.

Appointment Scheduling:

  • Assistance in scheduling appointments with the appropriate visa application centers or consulates.
  • Providing information on the location, timing, and requirements for the visa application submission.

Biometric Enrollment Assistance:

  • Guidance on the biometric data collection process, including fingerprints and photographs.
  • Ensuring clients are prepared for and informed about the biometric enrollment appointment.

Communication with Authorities:

  • Regular follow-ups with visa processing authorities to track the status of the application.
  • Addressing any additional requests for information or documentation promptly.

Interview Preparation:

  • Coaching and guidance for visa interviews, including potential questions and appropriate responses.
  • Mock interviews to help clients feel confident and prepared.

Visa Collection Support:

  • Assistance in collecting the visa once approved, including instructions on where and how to retrieve the visa document.

Continuous Updates:

  • Regular updates on the progress of the visa application.
  • Timely communication regarding any changes in visa policies or procedures.
