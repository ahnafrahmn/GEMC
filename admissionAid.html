ADMISSION AID SERVICES

We provide a comprehensive support from searching an appropriate subject area, university, and getting an admission to fulfill your dream.

Our admission aid supports include:

Admission Assistance:

  • Guidance on choosing the right academic program and institution based on the student's goals and preferences.
  • Assistance with the application process, including documentation and application submission.
  • Expert advice on meeting admission requirements and deadlines.

Language Proficiency Assistance:

  • Recommendations for language proficiency tests (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL) required for admission.
  • Preparation resources and guidance to achieve the necessary language scores.

Visa Counseling and VISA processing:

  • Comprehensive support in the visa application process, ensuring compliance with immigration regulations.
  • Assistance in preparing and organizing visa documentation.
  • Processing student visa, family visa, tourist visa, skill migration visa.

Financial Aid Counseling:

  • Information on available scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities.
  • Assistance in preparing scholarship applications and required documentation.
  • Advice on budgeting and managing finances during the study period.

Document Verification:

  • Thorough review of academic and personal documents to ensure they meet the admission and visa requirements.
  • Assistance in obtaining and verifying transcripts, recommendation letters, and other necessary documents.

Orientation Services:

  • Pre-departure orientation sessions for students, providing information on the destination country, culture, and academic expectations.
  • Assistance with travel arrangements and accommodation.

Post-Arrival Support:

  • Assistance in acclimating to the new environment, including local orientation and support services.
  • Ongoing support for any issues related to academics, visa compliance, or personal matters.

Career Counseling:

  • Guidance on career paths and opportunities available after completing the academic program.
  • Assistance in connecting with potential employers or networking events.

Updates and Communication:

  • Regular updates on the status of applications and visa processes.
  • Prompt communication regarding any changes in immigration policies or academic requirements.

Customized Services:

  • Tailored services based on individual needs and preferences.
  • Flexibility to address specific challenges or requirements unique to each student.
